Social media helps to connect the world all over, being established after the 1990’s.

In this investigation, the negative effects of social media were extensively studied, focusing on its impacts on the current generation and the reach that social media has across the world.

Due to the rapid surge in popularity of social media over the last decade, some problems have begun to surface, such as addiction, doom scrolling,  and reduction of attention span. These problems extend to the professional and personal lives of those impacted. Hence, we decided to study to what extent social media affects people worldwide and whether social media is a growing problem for current and upcoming generations.
